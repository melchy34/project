Site Plan Document

Site Name

Site Name: Melchy4U Adventures

Reason for Site Name: This name was selected because it conveys both environmental consciousness ("Green") and the fun, adventurous aspect of cycling ("Cycle"). It is memorable and evokes a sense of eco-friendly adventure for outdoor cycling enthusiasts.

Optional Domain Availability: melchy4Uadventures.com

Site Purpose

Site Purpose: Melchy4U Adventures is designed to provide a platform for cycling enthusiasts. It will offer information about eco-friendly cycling tours, provide details on bike rentals, offer guided adventure experiences, and serve as a hub for cycling enthusiasts to connect and share tips.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "Where can I find eco-friendly bike tours in the area?"

Scenario 2: "What should I pack for a multi-day cycling adventure?"

Color Schema

The color schema of the website will incorporate eco-friendly tones to reflect the site's environmental focus. The following colors will be used:

Main Colors:
Green (#4CAF50) - Used for headings and call-to-action buttons
Tomato (#FF6347) - Used for accents, links, and button hover effects

Typography

Font 1: Arial, sans-serif - This will be the primary font for body text and general content.

Font 2: Georgia, serif - This will be used for headings to give a more sophisticated and impactful feel.
